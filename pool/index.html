How it works

Swap

Send MINIMA or USDT and receive the other token at the current reserve price. A 1% fee stays in the pool and the price adjusts automatically with every trade.

Add liquidity

Deposit MINIMA and USDT in the current pool ratio. Receive LP tokens representing your proportional share of both reserves.

Remove liquidity

Return your LP tokens at any time and receive your proportional share of both reserves, including all trading fees accumulated since your deposit.

For liquidity providers

Earn fees passively

The 1% trading fee stays in pool reserves. As volume accumulates, each LP token is redeemable for a growing share of the pool.

Fungible LP tokens

LP tokens are standard fungible tokens. Your ownership percentage equals your LP balance divided by the total circulating supply.

No lock-up

Remove your liquidity at any time. There is no vesting schedule, no withdrawal delay, and no minimum holding period.

Impermanent loss risk

If the MINIMA/USDT price diverges significantly from your entry point, the pool's rebalancing may reduce your value versus simply holding both assets.

Trust properties

  • No admin key: no operator can pause, upgrade, or drain the pool
  • Non-custodial: funds are controlled by the pool script, not any wallet or server
  • No fee receiver: fees stay in reserves and accrue to LPs automatically
  • Script-enforced: every swap, deposit, and withdrawal is validated on-chain by the contract
  • Open source: all contract code and MiniDAPP source is publicly auditable on GitHub
  • No upgrade path: migration to a new version is explicit and user-led, not operator-triggered
